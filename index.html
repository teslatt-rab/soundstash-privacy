SoundStash Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

SoundStash is a 100% offline music player. We do not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data.

Permissions We Use

Storage: To read music files on your device only. Files never leave your device.

Foreground Service: To keep music playing in the background.

Notifications: To show playback controls in your notification shade.

Data Storage

All data such as playlists, favorites and settings is stored locally on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob for ads. Google may collect data per their own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

Lyrics

When fetching lyrics, your song title and artist name are sent to lrclib.net. Lyrics are then saved offline on your device.

Contact

For any privacy questions please contact us at your app email address.
