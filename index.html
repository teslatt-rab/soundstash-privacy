Privacy Policy
SoundStash is a 100% offline music player. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

❌ Personal Info

No name, email or account required

❌ Location

We never access your location

❌ Analytics

No usage tracking or analytics

❌ Music Files

Your music never leaves your device

Permissions We Request

📁 Storage Access

To scan and play music files stored on your device. Files never leave your device.

🔔 Notifications

To show music playback controls in your notification shade and lock screen.

⚙️ Foreground Service

To keep music playing when the app is in the background.

Local Storage

All app data including playlists, favorites, play counts, settings and lyrics is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

Third Party Services

📢 Google AdMob

Used for displaying ads in the free version. Google may collect advertising data per their privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

🎤 lrclib.net

Used for fetching song lyrics. When you fetch lyrics, your song title and artist name are sent to lrclib.net. Lyrics are saved offline after the first fetch.
